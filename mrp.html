Done MRP gives manufacturing companies reliable control of production and logistics, without unnecessary complexity. Planning, execution, inventory, purchasing, and shop floor operations — all in one system. Financial processes stay in Finago through a dedicated integration. Clear responsibilities, consistent data, predictable days.

What gets in the way

  • Loss of control across production, inventory, and logistics
  • Manual replanning when demand or capacity changes
  • Limited visibility into production orders, material availability, and lead times
  • Weak traceability of items, revisions, and goods movements
  • ERP systems that are broad in scope but difficult to adapt to operational needs
  • Disconnected systems for sales, production, and finance

Done MRP focuses on what matters in day-to-day manufacturing and integrates directly with financial systems like Finago.

Functionality

Manage raw materials, work in progress, and finished goods with clear stock levels, locations, and movements.
Maintain traceability across materials, revisions, and production steps. Support controlled changes without disrupting ongoing work.
Create, release, and follow up production orders. Track progress by operation and maintain an accurate overview of production status.
Maintain structured BOMs and routings with revision control. Updates are traceable and consistently applied throughout planning and execution.
Plan and adjust production based on demand, confirmed orders, and available capacity. Changes are reflected directly in schedules and production orders. Plan and balance machines, work centers, and labor capacity to identify constraints and support realistic schedules.
Full control over purchase orders. Generate purchasing suggestions based on demand and inventory levels. Follow up suppliers, lead times, and deliveries.
Custom build your own reports with drag-and-drop functionality. Custom built reports can easily be accessed with access control to allow employees to only see relevant information.
Manage daily shop floor activities including time registration, material consumption, and operation reporting. Give operators a clear view of their tasks and progress.

Integration with Finago

Done MRP integrates with Finago to ensure consistent and reliable data flow between the two systems.

  • Two-way sync of customers and suppliers
  • Two-way sync of Sales Orders and Invoices
  • Two-way sync of items, stock-levels and actual production costs
  • General Ledger transactions are automatically posted in Finago

This approach supports both operational teams and finance without overlap or duplication.
